My Journey

I am a **certified meditation and yoga teacher** with over **20 years of experience** in helping individuals achieve mental clarity and emotional balance. Throughout my journey, I have had the privilege of working with people from all walks of life, including India's **Olympic medalist Manu Bhaker**. I guided Manu through personalized meditation practices to help her **enhance focus, build resilience, and master mental strength**, contributing to her success at the Olympics. Currently based in **Rishikesh**, the yoga capital of the world, I am building a **community of like-minded individuals** who are eager to embark on their journey of self-discovery through meditation and yoga.
